HELPING KIDS GO TO SCHOOL

Red Sweater Project creates high quality middle and high schools for kids in rural East Africa.

Superman has his cape, and Zoro has his mask. For these kids, a school uniform is transformational. Putting on a red sweater means having the opportunity to learn, and to better their community.
